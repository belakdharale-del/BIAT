Strategic Selection: LightGBM

The selection of LightGBM as the champion model for BIAT's surveillance infrastructure is driven by its exceptional handling of large-scale banking data and its robustness against imbalanced classes.

In our context, Recall is the primary optimization objective. Missing a single risky client (False Negative) carries significantly higher institutional cost than a manual review of a low-risk client (False Positive). LightGBM's gradient-based one-side sampling (GOSS) allows us to maintain a high Recall of 0.9372 without degrading general Accuracy.
